If you do not pay

If you fail to pay this fine as ordered you will be liable for further penalties. This could include:

 

• Deductions from your earnings or benefits

• 50% increase in your fine

• Clamping, removal and sale of your vehicle

• Registering the account in the Register of Judgments, Orders and Fines (making it harder for you to get credit)

• A distress warrant being issued to the Court Bailiffs to seize your goods (incurring additional costs)

• Continued default - you may be sent to prison

 

What should I do if I change my name or address?

If you change your name or address you must tell your court immediately. You'll find your court's contact details by using the Find a court or tribunal service and searching for your court.
